Robotics (C++)


Autonomous Medical Robot (for needle insertion): Programmed a surgical robot system similar to ROSA ONE, which is utilized for minimally invasive brain surgery. The project was implementated through Frank Emika Panda Robot Arm in University of Toronto's Robotics Lab. Used Eigen and libfranka libraries in C++ to perform algebraic computations of vectors and matrices and control the robot arm.

Chhoti Ammi - A Kitchen Robot: A collaborative robot (Franka Emika's Panda Robot Arm) programmed to cook a typical Indian tiffin - paratha and sabzi. Built using the Franka Desk GUI and the fundamental of robotics such as motion planning, human-robot interactions, inverse kinematics, and understanding of joint-space/task-space/work-space, to ensure completion of the task.
